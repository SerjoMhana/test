About

Dont be busy be productive

Tamkeen organization for community development is a nonprofit and non governmental organization aims to build a classy, conscious community and develop institutions and build the ability of individuals.

Tamkeen organization seeks to achieve and sustain development for individuals and institutions. Also to build a qualified and effective civil community with a spirit of giving, cooperation and solidarity.
